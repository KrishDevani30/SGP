How to Use Title Checker

Follow these simple steps to verify your titles

1

Prepare Your Text File

Create a text file (.txt) containing your existing titles. Each title should be on a new line.

Make sure your text file is properly formatted with one title per line.
2

Upload Your File

Click the "Choose File" button or drag and drop your text file into the upload area on the home page.

3

Enter Title to Check

Type or paste the title you want to verify in the input field.

The check is case-insensitive, so "My Title" and "my title" are treated as the same.
4

Verify the Title

Click the "Verify Title" button to check if your title already exists in the file.

5

Review Results

The system will show you whether the title is unique or already exists. You can view all your previous checks in the Verification History section.
